Processing purposes and legal basis: Basic research such as that conducted by the MPI-EVA expands the (cultural) knowledge of our society. This means the research projects of the institute are activities in the public interest and meet the criteria laid down in Article 6 and 9 EU GDPR (in particular Article 6 Para. 1 Letter e EU GDPR).

Data archive: Data will be stored in a password protected electronic format for at least ten years (required by law, GDPR compliant).

Data protection: We do not collect identifying information such as your name, email address, or IP address. Your anonymity is assured; the researchers who have requested your participation will not receive any personal information about you. Anonymized data can be used as part of scientific contributions at research, teaching or information events and can be made available to other researchers online.

Right to complain to the supervisory authority: If you are of the opinion that the processing of your personal data is unlawful, you have the right to complain to a supervisory authority (Art. 77 GDPR).

Provision of the data: Your participation and your answers to the tasks in this study are completely voluntary. You can withdraw at any time by closing the browser window or tab.
